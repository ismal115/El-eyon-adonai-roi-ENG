Our Worship Services

Join our gatherings where we celebrate God's presence through praise, the Word, and fellowship. Each service is an opportunity to connect with God and the community.

We have services designed for different ages and spiritual needs, always maintaining the essence of Christ's gospel.

Dance Ministry

The dance ministry is an artistic and spiritual expression that is part of our services and special events. Through movement, we seek to honor God and convey His message visually and prophetically.

We participate in presentations during worship services, vigils and celebrations, incorporating styles such as prophetic dance, liturgical and creative dance.

Music Ministry

Our music ministry is fundamental to congregational worship. Musicians and singers prepare spiritually to guide the church in an atmosphere of sincere and edifying praise.

The team participates in services, vigils and special events, using various instruments and musical styles that exalt Christ's name.

Baptisms

We celebrate the sacrament of baptism as a step of faith and commitment to Christ. We offer baptismal preparation for adults, youth and children, explaining the spiritual meaning of this act.

We perform immersion baptisms in our baptismal pool during special services. Contact us to schedule your baptism or that of your loved ones.
