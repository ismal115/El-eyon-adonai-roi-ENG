About Us

What is Eyon Adonai Roi?

"We are the Pentecostal Evangelical Church Eyon Adonai Roi" A faith community rooted in Christ's love and guided by the Holy Spirit. Our name which means "Most High God, my Shepherd" reflects our essence: worshippers seeking God's presence and disciples committed to His Word.

Our Mission

To guide people to a personal encounter with Christ, form committed disciples and serve the community with love and compassion.

We seek to reflect Jesus' character in all we do, being light and salt in our environment.

Annual Bible Conference

In celebration of Bible Month in September, the Annual Bible Conference is held in the Dominican Republic, an event that seeks to deepen the study of God's Word and strengthen the congregation's faith. It brings together pastors, leaders and special guests in biblical theology, and offers teachings, workshops, worship and discussions focused on the importance and relevance of Scripture. In addition to being a learning space, the conference promotes unity, love for Bible reading and the spiritual growth of the church.
